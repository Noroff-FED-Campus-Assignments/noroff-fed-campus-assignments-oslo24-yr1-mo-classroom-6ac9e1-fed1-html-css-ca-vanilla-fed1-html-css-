    Product Details

    A modern, warm and weatherproof parka that protects you on the coldest days. It features our trusted Protection technology, so it's waterproof, breathable and windproof. In addition, it has a number of smart details, such as reflectors on the sleeves and knitted wrist cuffs. We've added removable faux fur on the hood, which both looks good and is functional as it creates a warm microclimate around your face when you remove the hood.

    Fiber content:

    Shell: 100% polyamide. Lining: 100% polyester. Lining 2: 100% polyamide. Insulation: 100% polyester (recycled). Faux fur: 85% acrylic, 15% polyester.

    Maintenance advice:

    Do not use fabric softener. Remove all loose parts before washing. Tumble dry until the garment is completely dry. Dry with dryer balls. Wash with similar colors.
